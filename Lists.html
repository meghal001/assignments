Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here is the recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs

Ingridients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1tbps Butter
  • 2tbsps cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a pan on medim heat.
  2. Gnetly mix the eggs and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once butter has melted mix cream and egg.
  4. Fold the eggs using a spatula from the edge of the pan every 20 secs.
  5. Take off from flame and serve hot
